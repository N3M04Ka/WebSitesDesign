This film is about a young man who is fed up with his boring life. He works in one corporation as an ordinary clerk. The most entertaining action he has is flipping through the catalog and choosing furniture for his house. After another business trip, returning home, he meets Tyler Durden, a soap merchant and manufacturer, on the plane. His outlook on life is completely different from that of a young man, he never did anything to improve himself. It's easier for Tyler to deal with the destruction of his personality. The young man accepts Tyler's philosophy and begins a life of self-destruction. After some time, they meet in a bar, have a drink and arrange a fight between themselves, which brings both of them pleasure. They decide to create their own Fight Club, where those who wish can let off steam. Their club quickly takes a good turn and becomes very popular. After one car accident, Tyler disappears without telling his friend. The most severe vandalism begins to occur in their club, and the young man decided to put an end to it.
