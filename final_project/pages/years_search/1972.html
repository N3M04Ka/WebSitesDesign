The Godfather (1972)

Premiere year: 1972

Country: USA

Genre:Movies / Criminal / Drama

Duration: 2:55

Premiere: March 24 1972

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

Actors: Marlon Brando / Al Pacino / James Caan / Richard Castellano / Robert Duvall / Sterling Hayden

In dark, Rembrandtian back rooms, Don Vito Corleone and his family serve, bless, and generally manage their friends in the real, vibrant, beautiful world. The father of the bride traditionally grants any wish, and petitions come from afar, when Don Corleone's daughter arranges a colorful wedding celebration on the street. Outside in 1945. Everything changes. Drugs are a new big industry, alluring and tempting, which other mafia families have already entered. Don Corleone is cautious, but his competitors are jealous...
